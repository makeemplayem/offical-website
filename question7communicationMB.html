how to create a floating magnet!

    you will need

  1. clay or playdough
  2. a pencil
  3. 2 ring magnets

instructions

  1. place the clay or playdough on a flat object
  2. place the pencil into the clay or playdough
  3. place one ring magnet trough the pencil
  4. place the other trough the pencil. make the magnets north to north or south to south.

artificial lightning!

    you will need

  1. adult supervision
  2. a plasma lamp
  3. a pencil
  4. 2 aligator clips

    instructions

  1. clip 1 of the aligator clip to the pencil
  2. place the pencil on to the plasma lamp
  3. put the other aligator clip within 0.2 inches of the other clip.
  4. turn on the plasma lamp
