BuildDash

BuildDash is a fun game were you get a prompt of what to build and you build it. can you become the best builder? this game is still in beta. also when you get into the game remeber to hit pause on the timer when you get on the game and unpause it when you start building. when you finish building take a screen shot and wait for the timer to reach zero to post it on this page (you have to download to play and allow popups)
